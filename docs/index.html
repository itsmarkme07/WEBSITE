About Me

I'm a passionate data analyst driven by curiosity and a love for uncovering stories hidden in data. With hands-on experience in Python, SQL, Excel, Power BI, and Tableau, I specialize in transforming raw information into clear, actionable insights that support smarter decisions.

What excites me most about data is discovering patterns that others might miss and turning those discoveries into intuitive dashboards, forecasts, or reports that make an impact. Whether it's through personal projects, case studies, or team collaborations, I thrive on solving problems and making complex data easier to understand.

Outside of analytics, you'll find me on hiking trails, on basketball or badminton court, diving into dramas and series, or experimenting with new ways to visualize data creatively or simply enjoying a good cup of coffee while brainstorming ideas.
